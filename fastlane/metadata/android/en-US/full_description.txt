GeoIP is an Android app that looks up IP address geolocation data. When
you open it, it automatically detects your current IP address and
shows where you are located (country, city, region, coordinates, ISP,
and organization). You can also manually enter any IP address to see
its location. The app displays all the details on the screen and shows
them on an interactive map. You can copy the location information with
one tap, or open it in an external map app like Google Maps. It
supports both IPv4 and IPv6 addresses, including compressed format and
IPv4-mapped IPv6 format. It also works with regular hostnames.
